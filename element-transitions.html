Students

Why Dreamschool?

The college process is too time consuming, costly, and random. Don't spend time and money touring schools across the country trying to find the right fit for you. By offering VR tours and student perspectives/stories of school, we want to help you find your dreamschool!

See what YOU want, when YOU want

We understand that everyone wants to get something different out of their time in college. With dreamschool, we enable students to see what THEY want to see at a campus, not what the tour guide wants you to see. Checkout a lecture, a football game, or just lounge in the Dining Hall. Whatever it is, its YOUR choice!

Find the right fit.

It's difficult to figure out which school is the right fit for you. To fix this problem, we provide currents student insight for each of our college campuses. Read stories, watch our vidoes, and checkout our school specs to find your dreamschool.
